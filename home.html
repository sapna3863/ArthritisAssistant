How It Works

Rehab Buddy is designed to assist you in recovery. Our system analyzes your movements and provides real-time feedback to ensure you are performing exercises correctly.

Whether you're recovering from an injury or looking to improve your physical health, Rehab Buddy is here to help you every step of the way.

Simply follow the guided exercises, and our system will track your progress, helping you stay motivated and on track.

About Computer Vision Detection

This assistant uses advanced machine learning via Teachable Machine (by Google) to help guide your exercises. Simply perform your exercises in front of your camera, and receive instant feedback on your form.

  • Real-time form detection
  • Easy and fast
  • Instant feedback

All processing happens in your browser - no videos are stored or shared.
