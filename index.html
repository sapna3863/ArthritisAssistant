How It Works

Arthritis Assistant analyzes your movements and provides real-time feedback to ensure you are performing exercises correctly, preventing inflammation.

Whether you have rheumatoid arthritis or osteoarthritis, Arthritis Assistant is here to help you strengthen your joints.

Simply follow the guided exercises, and our system will track your progress, helping you stay motivated and on track.

The Arthritis Foundation

The Arthritis Foundation is the nation's leading nonprofit organization dedicated to conquering arthritis, America's #1 cause of disability.

Founded in 1948, the Arthritis Foundation has been championing the fight against arthritis for over 75 years, supporting the nearly 60 million adults and hundreds of thousands of children in the United States affected by this disease.

Our Mission

The Arthritis Assistant is a model to bring innovative solutions to arthritis care, empowering patients with the tools they need for effective self-management.

How They Help

  • Fund medical research for more effective treatments and a cure
  • Provide educational resources and support programs
  • Advocate for improved healthcare policies and legislation
  • Connect people with arthritis to supportive communities
  • Host events and programs to raise awareness and funds

Learn more at www.arthritis.org
